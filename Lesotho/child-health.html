Key Findings

74% of children aged 12-23 months are fully vaccinated with basic antigens. Only 43% are fully vaccinated according to the national schedule. Less than 1% have received no vaccinations. Coverage varies by district—highest in Berea (61%) and lowest in Qacha's Nek (23%).
