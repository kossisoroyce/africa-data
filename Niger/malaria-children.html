Key Findings

29% of children aged 6-59 months tested positive for malaria by RDT nationally. Prevalence varies dramatically by region—from 2% in Agadez to 46% in Dosso. 11% of children have moderate-to-severe anaemia (Hb <8 g/dl). Southern regions have much higher malaria burden than northern desert regions.
