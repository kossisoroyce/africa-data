Key Findings

The 2022-23 MIDS includes a new mental health module using standardized, validated instruments. Anxiety is measured using the GAD-7 (Generalized Anxiety Disorder 7-item scale) and depression using the PHQ-9 (Patient Health Questionnaire 9-item). Scores are categorized by severity.

GAD-7 Anxiety Scale

Score interpretationDescription
0-4Minimal anxiety
5-9Mild anxiety
10-14Moderate anxiety
15-21Severe anxiety

PHQ-9 Depression Scale

Score interpretationDescription
0-4Minimal depression
5-9Mild depression
10-14Moderate depression
15-19Moderately severe
20-27Severe depression

Anxiety Prevalence

The survey reports the percentage of women and men aged 15-49 with anxiety symptoms (GAD-7 score ≥6) by various characteristics including age, education, and residence.
