Key Findings

29% of women aged 15-49 have ever experienced physical violence since age 15. 14% have experienced sexual violence. 8% experienced violence during pregnancy. Among ever-married women, 35% have experienced IPV. 50% of women who experienced violence never sought help or told anyone.
