Key Findings

Outpatient curative care for sick children is one of the most widely available services in Ethiopia, provided by 89% of facilities. However, quality of care is concerning: all three general danger signs were assessed in only 4% of observed consultations. 86% of children with diarrhoea without dehydration were treated with oral antibiotics, and 85% of children with respiratory illness received oral antibiotics.
