Key Findings

Only 20% of Ethiopia's health facilities offer all basic services (outpatient curative care, child vaccination, family planning, ANC, and delivery). While 54% have regular electricity and 53% have improved water, only 5% have supportive management practices. Medroxyprogesterone (Depo Provera) injections and mebendazole were the most widely available essential medicines.
