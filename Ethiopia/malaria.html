Key Findings

70% of Ethiopian health facilities offer malaria diagnosis and/or treatment. 66% can test for malaria by RDT, but only 21% have microscopy capacity. Only 61% of facilities that treat sick children have any first-line antimalarial available. Just 9% of facilities treating sick children can be considered fully ready to diagnose and treat malaria. Among children observed, only 3% were diagnosed with malaria.
