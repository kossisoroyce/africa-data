Key Findings

56% of Ethiopian health facilities offer delivery services, but only 42% provide 24/7 service. Basic Emergency Obstetric and Newborn Care (BEmONC) is available in only 18% of facilities, and Comprehensive EmONC (CEmONC) in just 8%. Only 35% of facilities have newborn resuscitation equipment (bag and mask), and 28% have a resuscitation table. Caesarean section is available at only 12% of facilities.
