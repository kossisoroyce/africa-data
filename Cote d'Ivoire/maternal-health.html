Key Insight

84% of births in Côte d'Ivoire are now attended by skilled health personnel, up from 59% in 2012. Urban areas have near-universal skilled attendance (95%), while rural coverage is 74%. Education significantly impacts delivery care—96% of women with secondary education deliver with skilled assistance compared to 75% of those with no education.
