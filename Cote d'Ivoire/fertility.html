Key Insight

Fertility in Côte d'Ivoire has declined from 5.0 in 2012 to 4.3 in 2021. Rural areas have 59% higher fertility than urban areas (5.4 vs 3.4). The Denguélé district has the highest TFR at 6.3, while Lagunes has the lowest at 3.9.
