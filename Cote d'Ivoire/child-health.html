Key Insight

Only 43% of children aged 12-23 months are fully vaccinated in Côte d'Ivoire. There is a stark wealth gap—63% of children in the wealthiest households are fully vaccinated compared to just 19% in the poorest. Regional disparities are also significant, with Denguélé at only 9% coverage.
