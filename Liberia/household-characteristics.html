Key Findings

The 2022 Liberia LMIS surveyed over 5,000 households across 6 regions. Greater Monrovia is the most populous region. About 48% of the population lives in urban areas. The survey collected data on housing characteristics, ITN ownership and use, malaria testing and treatment, and ANC care.
