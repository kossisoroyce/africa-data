Neonatal and under-5 mortality rates, 1990-2024

Slow Progress

While under-5 mortality has declined from 213 in 1990 to 110 in 2024, Nigeria still has one of the highest child mortality rates in the world. Neonatal mortality remains stubbornly high.

Perinatal deaths

The stillbirth rate is 19 per 1,000 pregnancies, and the early neonatal mortality rate is 33 per 1,000 live births.

Regional variation

Under-5 mortality ranges from 14 in Kwara to 161 in Jigawa — an 11-fold difference between states.
