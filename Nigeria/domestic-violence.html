Underreported Crisis

Only 24% of women who experienced violence sought help. Most survivors suffer in silence, with few turning to formal services like police (3-4%) or healthcare providers.

Where survivors seek help

Among those who sought help: 65% turned to their own family, 37% to husband's family, only 3-4% to police.

Controlling behaviors

46% of women report partners who are jealous if they talk to other men. 12% experience 3+ controlling behaviors.
