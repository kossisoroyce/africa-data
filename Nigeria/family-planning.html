Long-acting Methods Lead

Implants have become the most popular modern method among married women in Nigeria, followed by injectables. This shift toward long-acting methods reflects improved access and provider training.

Public vs private sources

75% of injectable users and 88% of implant users obtain their method from public sources, while 82% of male condom users rely on private sources.

Decision-making patterns

67% of married women participate in family planning decisions — either jointly with their partner (29%) or mainly by themselves (37%).
