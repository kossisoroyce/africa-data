The Sanitation Crisis

One in five Nigerians still practices open defecation. In rural areas, this rises to 31%. Safe management of human waste remains a critical challenge.

Water availability

66% of the population had sufficient quantities of drinking water in the last month. Urban areas fare better at 71% vs rural 61%.

Who collects water?

For households without water on premises, adult women (35%) and adult men (32%) share water collection duties roughly equally.
