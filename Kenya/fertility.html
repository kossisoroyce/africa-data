Key Findings

Kenya's total fertility rate is 3.4 births per woman in 2022, continuing a long decline from 6.7 in 1989. There is a significant urban-rural gap—TFR is 2.8 in urban areas vs 4.3 in rural areas. 15% of women aged 15-19 have begun childbearing. Fertility varies widely by county and education level.
