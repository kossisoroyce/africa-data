Key Findings

95% of women in Gabon receive at least one ANC visit, and 78% receive 4+ visits. 90% of births are attended by skilled personnel and 89% occur in health facilities. The C-section rate is 12%. Coverage is highest in Libreville (96% skilled delivery) and lowest in Ogooué-Ivindo (72%). Rural areas have lower coverage than urban areas.
