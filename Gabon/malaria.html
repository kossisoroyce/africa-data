Key Findings

Only 21% of households in Gabon have at least one ITN, and just 16% of the population has access to an ITN. However, among those with access, usage is high—68% of children under 5, 63% of pregnant women, and 59% of the general population slept under an ITN. Malaria prevalence among children 6-59 months is 13% by RDT. Treatment was sought for 76% of febrile children.
