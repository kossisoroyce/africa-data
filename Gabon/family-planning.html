Key Findings

22% of married women in Gabon use modern contraception. Male condoms are the most popular method (8%), followed by pills (5%) and injectables (4%). Unmet need for family planning is 27%, highest among young women aged 15-19 (32%). Only 45% of demand for family planning is satisfied with modern methods.
