Key Findings

57% of women in union and 98% of men worked in the past 12 months. 50% of women who earned money decide how to use it themselves. 89% of women and 90% of men own a mobile phone. 75% of women and 73% of men have used a bank account or mobile money for financial transactions. 29% of women and 33% of men think wife-beating is justified for certain reasons.
