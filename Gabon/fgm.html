Key Findings

FGM prevalence in Gabon is very low, with approximately 1% of women aged 15-49 having undergone FGM. The vast majority of Gabonese (over 90% of both women and men) believe that FGM should be discontinued. This reflects the country's strong stance against the practice, which is not traditional to most Gabonese ethnic groups.
