Key Findings

Gabon has high literacy rates—88% of women and 94% of men are literate. 62% of women and 58% of men have secondary education. TV is the most common media source (82% of women, 85% of men). 75% of women and 73% of men have used a bank account or mobile money. 62% of women and 72% of men have used the internet in the past 12 months.
